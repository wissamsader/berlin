Deconstruct. Rebuild. Respect the flavor.

LMATBAKH opened on Armenia Street in 2025 and filled its chef's table within months. Chefs Christine Saad and Elie Harb take the dishes Lebanon knows by heart — mutabbal, moghrabieh, kibbeh — and rebuild them with French technique, live, a meter from your seat. Two seatings a night, reservations required, and a menu 'according to the chef' that changes as the market does.
